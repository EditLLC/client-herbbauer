HERB BAUER Camping

Outside is what we’re all about! Whether it’s sitting outdoors in the shade in a comfortable chair (with a cold drink nearby), or doing battle with the mountain while scaling a High Sierra peak, we’ve got you covered. We have the best selection of what you’ll need for whichever extreme of the outdoors you’re into. Stop in and shop our camping, backpacking and climbing department. Check-out our stoves, lanterns, ice chests, tents, water purification options, tables, chairs, …. The list goes on and on, from heavy duty to ultra light. Our experts will help you select the perfect gear for your next trip outdoors. We also have the best selection of rental gear in the Valley. Stop in, before you go out!

HERB BAUER Hunting

We have the largest selection of shotguns, rifles and handguns in-stock in the Valley. We are Master dealers, Stocking dealers, Factory Direct dealers, Premier dealers, Full-line dealers or armorors for most manufacturers. We have a knowledgeable, experienced staff that can help you select the right self defense or hunting firearm for your needs.

We have Hunter Safety classes and offer Initial & Renewal CCW classes plus Basic Handgun Training and New Shooter Firearm Orientations. We buy, sell and repair used guns. We have an excellent selection of the best holsters, optics, safes, Modern Sporting Rifles and accessories, etc, etc in the Valley. And, we have a large selection of non-lead hunting ammo and reloading supplies.

We provide a 10% Gift Card back discount for current NRA/CRPA/GOCA/CDA/DU/CWA/RMEF/WTF/Boy Scout card carrying members. Also, ask about our price matching policy. We do layaways and we have no credit card up-charges on purchases.

Classes: Hunter Safety, Initial & Renewal CCW, Basic Handgun Training, New Shooter Firearm

Orientation (Call 559-435- 8600 for more information)

HERB BAUER Skiing

HERB BAUER Fishing

We specialize in Fishing Gear for the central Valley and the Sierras. Whether it’s Spin fishing for High Sierra Trout, Bait casting for Large Mouth Bass or Trolling the depths for a monster Brown Trout, the correct gear is the foundation of a great experience on the water. We have the largest selection of Fly Fishing and Fly Tying supplies in the central Valley, which includes rod, reels, flies, leaders, vests, waders, vises, hooks, feathers, furs, etc.

Your experience on the Water is just as important to us as it is to you and we will be glad to assist you in making the correct choices. We have free Fly Casting classes for the beginner or if you need help fixing a particular casting problem. We offer Fly Tying Classes in the Winter months.

Classes: Basic Fly Fishing-Casting and Fly Tying (Call 559-435- 8600 for more information)
