HERB BAUER hunting

Hunting Safety Classes
Hunter Safety Classes are held year-round every other Saturday from 9:00 - 5:30. The Cost is $20.00 per person. Each class holds 50 people and they usually fill up one month in advance. The questions at the end of each chapter of the Hunter Safety Study Guide need to be completed before attending class. This book is free to persons who have pre-paid for the class. Call Herb Bauer Sporting Goods today to reserve a spot!

HERB BAUER Fishing

Fly fishing classes


Herb Bauer's own Pete Willison teaches the techniques and skills necessary to get anyone started in the sport of fly fishing. Pete not only teaches the handling and casting of a fly rod, he explains fly selection, tying and the basic knots necessary to secure your rig. Sign-ups are on a first-come, first-serve basis so call or come in today to get started.
