Herb Bauer Cycling

Herb Bauer Cycling offers the largest selection of bicycles in the valley! Featuring bmx, mountain, road, hybrid, cruiser, and much more.

We stock a full line of parts, accessories, and clothing. Whether you are an enthusiast or beginner, Herb Bauer Cycling will have the items to fit your needs.

We are the premier bmx dealer in the central valley. Come see our selection of complete street, dirt and race bikes. Also, check out our huge parts department to customize your bike or build from the ground up with our frame selection.

Herb Bauer is also the mountain bike destination for all levels of riders. We carry the top performing brands in the industry and have the expert knowledge and service to keep your bike on the trail.

Get on the road again with the full range of road bikes on our sales floor. Stop on by and see some of the finest bikes available today.

We offer lifetime free basic tune-ups, bike fitting, suspension tuning, and just all around good advice with all new bike purchases.
